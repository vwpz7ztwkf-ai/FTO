Decision Map

Lead vs. confirmation
Person hitConfirm identity, active status, pickup/extradition, and originating-agency confirmation before action based on the hit.
Plate hitConfirm plate/state/type, vehicle description, VIN when needed, and originating-agency hit confirmation before seizure/recovery.
RO hitRegistered owner is a lead. Identify the driver and confirm any person hit separately.
MyCaseUse for court context only. It does not replace IDACS/NCIC, POR, dispatch, court, or originating-agency confirmation.
